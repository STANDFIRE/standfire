Selection of ground cells

Principle

  • Select the chosen cells directly with the mouse. The selected cells are drawn in red color.

Selection / deselection

  • To (de)select a cell, double click on it with the left button of the mouse.
  • To (de)select a group of cells: draw a selection rectangle arround them with the right button of the mouse.

Information for one or several cells

  • Select the cell(s) while holding down the Control key.

Zoom

  • Zoom in: draw a zoom rectangle arround the zone of interest with the left button of the mouse.
  • Zoom out: click on the image with the right button

Move in the image

  • Draw a translation vector on the image with the mouse while holding down the control key.
