Simmem

Simmem is a model that manages several forests (régions of Orléans and Vercors, France). Each stand grows with a specific Capsis model according to the species and forest structure it contains. Simmem was first connected to Sylvestris and Fagacées. It is part of the Forgeco ANR project.
