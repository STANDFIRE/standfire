Stretch

Authors

Marilyne Laurans, Jean Dauzat (Cirad AMAP), Cécile Madelaine, Grégoire Vincent, Lucile Soler (IRD AMAP), Degi Harja Asmara (ICRAF Indonesia)

Description

A rewriting of the Sexi-FS model within Capsis with a connection to the AMAP light model by Jean Dauzat (Mir-Musc-Radbal) and other evolutions (see The Sexi-FS project).

Stretch is a multi species distance dependent tree model that can be parametrized for various species. The crown shapes of the trees are changing according to available space and available light.
