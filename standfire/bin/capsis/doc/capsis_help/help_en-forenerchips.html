ForEnerChips

ForEnerChips is not a growth model. It’s a tool that aims to simulate the production chain of forest wood chips for energy. It processes resources produced by sylvicultural interventions of forest growth simulations. It simulates all steps to pruduce wood chips from standing trees, and to deliver these to the heating plant.

It is at first compatible to the FAGECEES simulator, but it is built to be easily made compatible with other simulators.

It doesn't work in the interface mode, but only from scripts.
