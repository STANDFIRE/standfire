The visualization in Biomechanics

Cartographic Viewer 2D

This viewer provides a view of the stand from above.

After loading application, it allows displacement of tree tops to be visualized. A tree is represented by a green point which size is proportional to the tree diameter at breast height. Tree top is represented by a red point of constant size. Both are linked by a red straight in order to give a displacement vector associated to each tree.

Mouse functions

  • Left button:

    Selection of a rectangular zone = zoom in on this zone.
    Double-click on a green point = selection of the tree and opening of the Tree Viewer.

  • Right button:

    Click anywhere = zoom out.
    Selection of a rectangular zone = selection of contained trees and opening of the Tree Viewer on these trees.

  • No button :

    Displacement with SHIFT pressed = pointed coordinates.
