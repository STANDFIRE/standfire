The visualization in Biomechanics

Tree Viewer

This viewer provides a 2D view of tree shape.

This shape (could be 3D) is done by an orthogonal projection on a vertical plane (zOx') for which orientation according to (Ox) axis can be chosen (with the slide bar or the text field)...

The one who views the tree look at the perpendicular of the projection plane. He's represented by the red triangle. Tree shape is drawn as the succession of its Growth Units for which diameter is here amplified...

The two buttons of the top give an graphic access to the distribution of compressive stresses along the stem and to the repartition of elementary contributions to basal bending moment, respectively...

Mouse functions

  • Left button:

    Selection of a rectangular zone = zoom in on this zone.

    Double-click on the centre of a Growth Unit = selection of the GU and opening of an inspector on its state.

  • Right button:

    Click anywhere = zoom out.

    Selection of a rectangular zone = selection of contained GU and opening of an inspector on their state.

  • No button :

    Displacement with SHIFT pressed = pointed coordinates.
