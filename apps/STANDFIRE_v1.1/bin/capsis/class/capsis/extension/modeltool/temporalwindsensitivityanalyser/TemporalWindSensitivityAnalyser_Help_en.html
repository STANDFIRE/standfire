Dialog with the Analyser FOREOLE

Click on the first Parameters button to adjust biomechanical parameters to use:

    - wood volumic density

    - its modulus of elasticity

    - ratio between crown mass and stem mass

    - drag coefficient used to evaluate wind forces

These parameters are species dependent and will be used along the entire analysis (i.e. for each step).

Click on the second Parameters button to adjust wind loading parameters. The wind speed can be specified (defined at stand edge at 10 m above the ground). Calculations are automatically made considering the tree population located inside the stand. Parameters associated to wind profile have to be modified with caution...

Thus, the Launch button executes the calculation of the mechanical state of each tree submitted to the wind profile above defined, for each visible step. Moreover, this calculation is automatically followed by a damage analysis based on failure criteria at the tree level...

The View button opens a graph representing the temporal evolution of the amount of partial wind damage relative to number of trees and to volume of wood in the population.

For more details on wind profile, method of structures computation and failure criteria (...), consult References of  FOREOLE Model.
