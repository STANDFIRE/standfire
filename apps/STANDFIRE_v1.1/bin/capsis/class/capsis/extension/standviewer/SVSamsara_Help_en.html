Principle

View the spatialized stand under the reference step on a map. This viewer is based on the Simple viewer and has the same main features.

Each tree is represented by a point at its real position in the stand. The crown is represented according to the chosen options, for example with a color corresponding to a given height strata. The ground cells are colored with a gray level related to the amount of light received during the last radiative balance calculation.
