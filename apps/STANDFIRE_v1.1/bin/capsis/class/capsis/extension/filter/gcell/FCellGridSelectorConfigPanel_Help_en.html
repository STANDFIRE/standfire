Ground cells selection

 Capsis Help

Principle

Select the needed cells directly with the mouse. The selected cells appear in red.

Selection / deselection

  • To (de)select a cell, double-click on it with the left button,

  • to (de)select a group of cells, draw a selection rectangle arround the cells with the right button.

Information about one or more cells

  • Select the cell(s) while holding down the Control key.

Zoom

  • Zoom in: draw a zoom rectangle arround the zone of interest with the left button,

  • zoom out: click on the image with the right button.

Translation on the image

  • Draw a translation vector on the image while holding down the Control key.
