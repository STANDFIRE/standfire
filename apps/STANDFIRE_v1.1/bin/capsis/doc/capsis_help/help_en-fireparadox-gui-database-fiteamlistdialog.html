TEAM EDITION

All team objects stored in the database appear in this list.

It contains teams names and the mention if the team is desactivated or not.

Control buttons are:

  • Modify: to modify an existing team
  • Add: to add a new team
  • Desactivate/Reactivate: to desactivate or reactivate an existing team
  • Close: to close the window

Desactivate action is usefull to denied access to a team without deleting their own data.
