Individual Thinning

Principle

Select the ids of the trees to be cut: choose the ids in the left side list and move them into the right side list. When your choice is made, press Ok.

Selection of the trees

To select a tree in the list, click on its identifier.

Then possibly:

  • click on another id while pressing the Shift key to select a block of contiguous ids
  • click other individuals in the list while holding the Control key to select trees which ids are not contiguous

Move from one list to the other

  • Add the left side selected trees to the right side list
  • Remove the selected trees from the right side list
  • Add all the trees from the left to the right
  • Remove all the trees from the right
  • Sort both lists in ascending order
