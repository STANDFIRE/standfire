Simple viewer

Principle

View the spatialized stand associated to a given step o, a map. Each tree is represented by a point at its real position in the stand. A label can show its numerical identifier (use the Preferences icon).

Information about one or several trees

Choose a selection mode in the proposed list, then select the tree(s):

  • To select a tree, double-click on it with the left button.
  • To select a tree group, draw a selection rectangle with the right button.

Zoom

  • Zoom in: draw a selection rectangle on the chosen zone with the left button.
  • Zoom out: click on the image

Moving on the image

  • Draw a translation vector on the image with the mouse and hold the Control key at the same time.
