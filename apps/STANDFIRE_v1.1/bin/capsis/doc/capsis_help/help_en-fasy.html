Module Fasy

Auteur

Grégory Deceliere, Cemagref, Domaine des Barres, 45290 Nogent-sur-Vernisson, FRANCE

Objectifs

Coupling methods applied to the Fagacées (Fa) and Sylvestris (sy) growth models.

Description

This module proposes two original methods linking several growth models of mono-specific even-aged tree stands. It allows here to simulate the growth of oak-Scots pine mixed stands.
