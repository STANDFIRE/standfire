This intervener is based on RDIAutoThinner, allowing one to use this thinner independently on the oaks and the pines. When the intervention is applied, the stand is duplicated. Pines are removed from one stand and oaks are removed from the other, yielding pure species stands. RDIAutoThinner is then used on each stands with different parameters. The two resulting stands are then merged.

Fill the targe RDI values and the corresponding thinning coeficients. Reports tabs shows corresponding results after intervention.

Warning : values are per Ha, contrary to the fasy initial dialog box
