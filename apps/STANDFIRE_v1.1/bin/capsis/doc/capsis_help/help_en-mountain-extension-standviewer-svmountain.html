Mountain viewer

Principle

View the Mountain stand under a given simulation step on a map. This viewer is based on the Simple viewer and has the same main features.

Each tree is represented by a point at its real position in the stand. A label can show its numerical identifier.

According to the chosen options, the crown can be rendered in different ways (colors related to height strata, outline or transparency).

The ground cells can be colored with gray levels depending on the amount of light received during the last phase of radiative balance calculation or with a yellow color pointing out the regeneration spots.

A hidable caption shows the directions of the bottom of the slope and the north. The scale is represented on the map by mesuring the side of a ground cell.
